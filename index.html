Bang for your buck? Candidate Ad spend in the Democractic primary correlated with votes and delegates

Taking total ad spend by 4th of March 2020, as reported by CNN, and the number of delegates and votes, as reported by Wikipedia as of 0820 UK time, we can look and see how directly ad spending correlates with voter preferences.

##          candidates spend delegates  votes

## 2        Tom Steyer 210.0         0  77236
## 3      Donald Trump  60.0        NA     NA

## 5    Pete Buttigieg  36.0        26 173466

## 7     Amy Klobuchar  17.0         7 102406

## 9     Tulsi Gabbard   5.5         1  16480

Spend v Delegates

Safe to conclude there is no strong relationship

Spend v Votes

Again, it is safe to conclude there is no strong relationship
